About Our Institution

Government Polytechnic College, Perinthalmanna, stands as a leading light of technical training and academic excellence in the region. Since its inception, the college has been committed to providing premium education, fostering innovative engineering skills, and producing highly capable, ethical technicians who lead industries.

Equipped with modern infrastructural labs, an expansive digital library, and mentored by highly experienced educational faculty, we ensure a comprehensive learning environment that matches the changing trends of global technology.
