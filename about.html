About Our Institution

Shaping standard engineering professionals since 1961

Institutional Overview

Government Polytechnic College, Perinthalmanna, is one of the premier technical education centers in Malappuram. The institution offers highly sought-after engineering diplomas designed to instill rigorous analytical skills and hand-on technological capabilities.

Our Vision

To be a globally recognized center of excellence in technical education, preparing highly creative and competent technocrats who address industrial and societal challenges with standard ethics.

Our Mission

To establish a state-of-the-art learning environment by upgrading laboratories and implementing hands-on training methodologies, reinforcing professional ethics and environmental awareness while facilitating industrial partnerships.

A Legacy of Education (Since 1961)

Established in the historic year of 1961, our polytechnic college was created with the foundational vision of providing critical vocational and technical knowledge in an era focused on national growth and engineering self-reliance. Over the decades, we have evolved from a modest local technical center into a full-scale, highly integrated engineering diploma college.

We take pride in our huge alumnus network who occupy prestigious technical positions in top corporations around the globe, testament to the quality training, persistent dedication, and technical integrity they received right here.

Academic & Campus Facilities

🔬 Advanced Labs

Every department features highly upgraded, safety-compliant laboratories that feature standard industrial testbeds and tools for actual hands-on learning.

📚 Centralized Library

Houses thousands of technical volumes, reference encyclopedias, international science journals, and a digital computer lounge for online research portals.

🏠 Secure Hostel

Safe and comfortable lodging facility inside the campus for outstation candidates, providing clean dining, hygienic spaces, and strict supervision.

💻 Central Computing Center

A high-speed intranet lab equipped with standard CAD/CAM platforms, programming IDEs, and simulation software tools accessible for all courses.

🏸 Sports Complex

Nurturing holistic development with standard facilities for football, cricket, basketball, and a gymnasium to encourage fitness and coordination.

🚌 College Bus Service

Well-structured transport facilities connecting the campus with major nearby residential centers and railway hubs for comfortable daily commutes.
