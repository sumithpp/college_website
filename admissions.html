Eligibility Criteria

Standard Requirements

Admission to the diploma courses is primarily based on academic merit in secondary education. Candidates seeking entry must satisfy the following criteria:

  • Academic Qualification: Candidates must have successfully completed SSLC/10th Standard or equivalent examinations from a recognized educational board.
  • Core Subject Merit: Selection indices are calculated prioritizing marks obtained in Mathematics, Science, and English.
  • Domicile: Must satisfy the domicile residency regulations laid out by the State Board of Technical Education.
  • Age Limit: No restrictive upper age limits apply for registering for general courses.

Application Timeline & Process

1. Online Registration

Fill out the online application portal via the centralized single-window allocation system. Select your preferred choices.

2. Verification of Documents

Submit or upload certified copies of grade sheets, category status, and residency certificates for online validation.

3. Publication of Rank Lists

Check the trial allotment and final ranks calculated automatically based on state-wide marks standardization.

4. Seat Allotment & Admission

Report directly to the allotted college along with original transcripts and mandatory fees to complete the enrollment.
